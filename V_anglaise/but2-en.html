Reflective analysis
Challenges faced

I had never done cybersecurity before this skill. All the protocols, cracking techniques and even basic security principles were completely unknown to me. Every exercise required a huge amount of research just to understand the topic before I could even start working on it.

How I overcame it

A lot of work, thorough research, and help from friends with prior knowledge. I also made heavy use of Kali Linux to practice directly on virtual machines playing both attacker and target roles. In this field, hands-on practice is the best way to learn. The labs let me experience both attack and defence, which helps understand both sides.

Transferable skills

Understanding how an attack works is exactly what it takes to become a pen tester. This skill gave me a real foundation for that. Knowing how to secure a network, choose the right crypto tools, understand common attack types and the defences to apply — all of this will serve me directly in an offensive or defensive cybersecurity role.

Pro situations // Surveillance & analyse du SI Security audit Security incident management
Reflective analysis
Challenges faced

What struck me most here was realising how complex properly monitoring a network really is. You have to pay attention to tiny details that are easy to miss in everyday life. Vulnerabilities in an Active Directory, for example, are genuinely hard to spot without knowing exactly what to look for.

How I overcame it

Research helped a lot, but it was mainly practice on root-me.pro and the labs with Kali Linux that really made me progress. Having to find real vulnerabilities on target machines taught me to see things differently — to think like someone trying to break in rather than someone building.

Transferable skills

To become a pen tester, knowing how to monitor is essential. You cannot find what you do not know how to detect. This skill taught me to read logs, spot abnormal behaviour, and understand how an attacker hides. That is exactly the kind of analysis done in a security audit or penetration test.
