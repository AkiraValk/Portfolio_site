// achievements

What I did well

  • English
    Learning English for me is more a passion than a chore. I started immersing myself in the language at age 10 and learned it entirely on my own. I reached C1 level through the LLCER English specialisation over two years in secondary school.
  • Programming
    I pick up Python easily, partly because my father works in the field and I grew up watching him code. I also spent time learning the basics with him. I then took the NSI specialisation for two years in secondary school and developed a real interest in programming.
  • Networking
    Once I discovered networking, I knew it was the right direction — it genuinely excites me. Moving from the theoretical diagram to the real configuration is challenging but fascinating. I am ready to keep learning through my studies and personal projects.

// progress

How do I keep improving?

I work a lot more at home and make sure to redo any project I did not fully understand or did not do well enough.

For any grade I consider insufficient, I go back to the class materials and exercises, and sometimes even to past exam papers I can find.

My aim is to understand my mistakes, fill in the gaps, and keep improving in every subject — even the most difficult ones.

// teamwork

Group work / Team integration

I am generally effective in group work, particularly as a project leader — distributing tasks fairly among members and setting clear deadlines.

As a team member I am equally reliable: I always complete my part before the deadline and before the final version, so as not to hold the group back.

I generally integrate well into work groups. When disagreements arise, I try to encourage dialogue and find common ground so that everyone is aligned and the project moves forward smoothly.

// autonomy

Independent work

I am an organised and methodical person when working alone. I manage my time well, plan tasks in advance of deadlines, and even if I cannot finish everything, I always push to complete as much as possible.

When I face difficulties, I first try to find the solution myself, then turn to online resources or ask for help.

I have a genuine passion for IT; my only real weakness seems to be electricity and electronics.

Areas of interest

I am especially productive on programming topics such as HTML, CSS and Python, as well as on theory — understanding how networks function and setting up small network environments.

On the configuration side, I know how to set up switches, basic servers, routers, as well as PCs, smartphones, and similar devices.

When the topic interests me, I am even more engaged and productive. That said, even on subjects I enjoy less, I always make the effort to give my best and work seriously, even when it is difficult.
