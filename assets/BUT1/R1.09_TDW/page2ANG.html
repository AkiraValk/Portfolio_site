Projects Completed

BLOB Project

Descriptions of these projects

In collaboration with CNRS, the National Centre for Space Studies (CNES) invited classes including my second year class, to conduct the same experiments as Thomas Pesquet, who was at that time on the ISS.
The experiment we conducted over a year consisted of raising a creature that is neither an animal, nor a fungus, nor a plant, but a living organism, in order to study it with several experiments carried out by groups.
I learned to manage and coordinate a working group for one year.
